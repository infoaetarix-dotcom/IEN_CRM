# Staff Guide — IEN Visa CRM (one page)

A quick guide for the consultancy team. No technical knowledge needed.

## Signing in
- Go to **`/login`** and enter your email + password (an admin creates your account).
- If you can't sign in, ask an admin to check your account is **Active**.

## The Dashboard
- **Leads this month**, **New today**, **Response rate (<1h)**, **Active sources** at a glance.
- Charts show where leads come from, where they are in your pipeline, and daily volume.
- **Recent leads** — click any to open it.

## Working a lead (Leads → click a lead)
- **Status** — move the lead through the pipeline: New → Contacted → In progress → Accepted / Rejected / Follow-up. Every change is recorded with your name and time.
- **Notes** — add internal notes (staff-only, never shown to the applicant). Notes can't be edited or deleted — they're a permanent record.
- **Send email** — pick a template, preview it (the applicant's name etc. are filled in automatically), and send. Every email is logged under **Message history** with success/failure.
- **Status history** — the full timeline of status changes.

## Finding leads (Leads page)
- **Search** by name, email, or phone.
- **Filter** by status, source, date, and (admins) by assigned agent.
- Results are paginated; use Prev/Next.

## Roles
- **Agent:** sees and works **only the leads assigned to you**.
- **Admin:** sees everything, assigns leads to agents, manages staff (**Agents** page), and edits email templates (**Templates** page).

## Admin tasks
- **Add staff:** Agents → *Add staff member* → name, email, temporary password, role. Share the password; they can sign in immediately.
- **Offboard staff:** use **Deactivate** (not delete) — this preserves the audit history while blocking their access.
- **Edit templates:** Templates → edit subject/body → Save. Use only the listed variables (e.g. `{{full_name}}`). The **welcome** template auto-sends to every new applicant.

## Good habits
- Contact new leads quickly — the dashboard tracks your <1-hour response rate.
- Use notes generously so the next person has context.
- Treat applicant data as confidential — it's sensitive personal information.

*Questions or issues → your system administrator.*
